Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026  ·  Operated by Jamie's Supply Company, LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how ShellSage AI ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Jamie's Supply Company, LLC, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at shellsage-ai.github.io or use our digital products.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Purchase information: When you buy a product through Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy, those platforms process your payment. We receive only non-sensitive order metadata (product purchased, amount, country) to fulfill delivery and track sales.
  • Email address: Provided voluntarily when purchasing or contacting us, used solely for order fulfillment and support.
  • Usage data: Aggregate, anonymized analytics (page views, referral source) collected via GitHub Pages default logging. No personal identifiers are retained.
  • Social platform data: If you interact with our Pinterest, X/Twitter, or Reddit presence, those platforms' privacy policies govern data collected there.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver purchased digital products
  • Respond to support requests
  • Publish content (pins, posts) on social platforms to promote our products — this is done via our own developer credentials and does not access your personal data
  • Analyze aggregate sales and traffic to improve products

3. Pinterest API Use

ShellSage AI uses the Pinterest API solely to publish product-related pins to our own Pinterest business account (ShellSage AI). We do not access, store, or process any Pinterest user data other than our own account's pin and board metadata. No user authentication or third-party Pinterest data is collected.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share information only with:

  • Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy: Payment and delivery processors — see their respective privacy policies
  • GitHub Pages: Static site hosting — see GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Law enforcement: Only when required by applicable law

5. Data Retention

Order metadata is retained for accounting and tax purposes as required by U.S. law. Email addresses used for support are deleted upon request.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at the address below.

7. Cookies

Our website (shellsage-ai.github.io) is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages and does not set first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by GitHub's infrastructure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
